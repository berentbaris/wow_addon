# Hardcore Classes Enhanced

**27 lore-based character archetypes for WoW Classic Hardcore.**

Pick an enhanced class — like Death Knight, Demon Hunter, Witch Doctor, or Mechano-Mage — and the addon tracks your challenge rules, gear restrictions, quest milestones, and talent requirements as you level. No guesswork, no spreadsheet alt-tabbing.

## What It Does

Each enhanced class comes with a unique combination of constraints that reshape how you play:

- **Equipment rules** — weapon type restrictions, armor limitations, specific item requirements that unlock at certain levels
- **Challenge modes** — Drifter (no hearthstone/bank), Ephemeral (no repairs), Partisan (no looted gear), Renegade (no quest rewards), Self-Made (craft your own gear), and more
- **Quest milestones** — themed quest chains tied to your class fantasy (Anti-demon, Pro-nature, Ironforge Loyalist, etc.)
- **Talent requirements** — specific talents you must take at specific levels, with spec-majority tracking
- **Companion/pet/mount rules** — required vanity pets, hunter pet species, and mount types

The addon monitors everything in real time: open the bank as a Drifter? Instant violation alert. Equip a looted sword as a Partisan? The panel lights up red. Repair your gear as an Ephemeral? Caught.

## The Panel

A persistent, draggable requirements panel shows your full checklist at a glance:

- Green checkmarks for rules you're meeting
- Red crosses for violations
- Grey entries for level-locked requirements you haven't reached yet
- Progress bar with completion percentage
- Hover tooltips with detailed check explanations

Access it via `/hce panel` or the minimap button.

## The 27 Classes

**Warriors:** Mountain King, Brewmaster, Demon Hunter
**Rogues:** Berserker, Warden, Runemaster
**Warlocks:** Pyremaster, Death Knight, Shadowmage
**Druids:** Druid of the Claw, Plagueshifter, Savagekin
**Hunters:** Buccaneer, Beastmaster, Mountaineer
**Shamans:** Spirit Champion, Witch Doctor, Spiritwalker
**Paladins:** Exemplar, Templar, Sister of Steel
**Priests:** Priestess of the Moon, Apothecary, Shadow Hunter
**Mages:** Bloodmage, Mechano-Mage, Warmage

Each class has a required race, talent spec, and a unique mix of equipment, challenge, and quest requirements — all designed around Warcraft lore.

## Slash Commands

- `/hce` — show all commands
- `/hce pick` — choose your enhanced class
- `/hce panel` — toggle the requirements panel
- `/hce progress` — print your checklist to chat
- `/hce quests` — check quest milestone progress
- `/hce settings` — open settings
- `/hce donate` — support the developer

## Installation

Drop the `HardcoreClassesEnhanced` folder into your `Interface/AddOns` directory. Works with WoW Classic Era and Season of Discovery (Interface 11507).
